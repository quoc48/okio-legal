Frequently Asked Questions

Is my voice recorded or stored? No. Your voice is converted to text on your device using Apple's speech recognition. Audio is never recorded or stored by Okio.
How does AI work in Okio? After your voice is transcribed, the text is sent to an AI service to extract individual tasks. Only text is sent, not your voice audio. No data is stored externally.
Is my data stored on a server? No. All your tasks are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
What is the free tier limit? Free users get 5 voice dumps per day with full access to all features. Okio Pro removes this limit for unlimited voice dumps.
