Description of Service

Okio is an ADHD voice companion app for iOS. It helps you voice-dump your thoughts, automatically extract tasks using AI, and work through them one at a time. All data is stored locally on your device.

Voice and AI Features

Okio uses Apple's speech recognition to transcribe your voice. Audio may be processed by Apple's speech services, similar to Siri. Transcribed text is sent to GPT-4o Mini via OpenRouter for AI task extraction.

  • Voice audio is never recorded or stored by Okio
  • Apple's speech services may process audio as part of system speech recognition
  • Only transcribed text is sent for AI processing. Your voice audio is never shared.
  • AI results may not always be accurate. Please verify extracted tasks
  • We are not responsible for inaccuracies in AI-generated tasks
